How to Request Account Deletion

To delete your MyCare App account and all related data, please send an email to the support address below:

Email: support@m16labs.com
Subject: Delete My Account – MyCare

Data Deletion Policy

All data related to the user will be deleted permanently upon processing the request. This includes:

  • Account credentials (phone number, email)
  • User profile and preferences
  • Consultation and activity logs

No personal or health-related data will be retained after deletion. If legally required, minimal metadata (such as invoice records) may be kept for up to 90 days for compliance purposes only.
